This three-month bootcamp is designed for intermediate-level participants seeking to advance their understanding of Artificial Intelligence (AI) concepts and techniques. The program covers advanced topics such as choosing the best way to apply AI in an industry and business, as well as redesigning key components of an organization's operating model. Participants will learn how to lead the creation of algorithms, software infrastructure, data pipelines, and experimentation platforms, and to ensure an ethical approach to AI while managing inherent risks. 

Throughout the program, participants will have the opportunity to apply their knowledge through hands-on exercises and projects, allowing them to gain practical experience and build their skills in this rapidly growing field. Whether you are a professional looking to upskill or an aspiring AI leader, this bootcamp will provide you with the knowledge and tools you need to succeed in this exciting and rapidly evolving field.
